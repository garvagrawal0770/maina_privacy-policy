Privacy Policy – Maina Medical Reminder App

Effective Date: July 30, 2025

Maina Medical Reminder App (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal data when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Data: Name, email (if provided), health reminders.
  • Device Data: Device model, OS version, app usage statistics.
  • Health Data: Medicine name, dosage, schedule — stored securely and locally or synced with your permission.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To schedule and notify you of reminders
  • To sync with smartwatches (if enabled)
  • To improve app performance
  • To comply with regional laws (GDPR, HIPAA, etc.)

3. Data Security

We use encryption and secure storage methods. No health data is shared with third parties without explicit consent.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with advertisers or third-party marketers. We only share anonymized, aggregated data for analytics, if at all.

5. Your Rights

  • European Union (GDPR): You can access, correct, or request deletion of your data.
  • USA (HIPAA & CCPA): You can request details of how your health information is used.
  • India (DPDP Act 2023): You have the right to know how your data is used and to withdraw consent.

6. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary for medical reminder services, unless legally required to retain it longer.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 (or 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect their data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted here with a revised “Effective Date.”
